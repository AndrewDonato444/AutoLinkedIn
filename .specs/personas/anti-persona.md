# Anti-Persona: Enterprise Sales Ops Manager

## Who This Is

- Manages outbound for a 50+ person sales team
- Needs multi-rep seat management, territory rules, approval hierarchies, CRM sync, compliance workflows
- Evaluates tools with procurement, legal, and IT involved
- Wants granular analytics dashboards, A/B testing frameworks, and reporting that exports to their BI tool

## Why They're Out of Scope

- Serving them would require building: multi-user permissions, team management UI, CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot), compliance audit logs, enterprise SSO, SLA guarantees
- Every feature they need adds complexity that slows down the primary persona's "5-minute review" workflow
- They'd push for a full dashboard/UI, which contradicts the automation-first principle
- Their buying cycle (6+ months, committee decision) doesn't match our distribution model (self-serve, immediate value)

## What to Say If Requested

"GojiBerry Auto is built for founders and small teams who run their own outbound. For enterprise sales ops needs (multi-rep management, CRM sync, compliance workflows), GojiBerry's core platform may be a better fit — or a dedicated sales engagement platform like Outreach/Salesloft."
